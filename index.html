ABOUT HISHOT

HIMTI Bina Nusantara is organizing an annual event called HISHOT, which stands for HIMTI Seminar-Workshop and Study Tour. The event is designed to enrich participants' knowledge and experience, and this year's focus is on the theme of "From Cyber Security to Cyber Resilience". By joining and participating in HISHOT 2024, the community is given an opportunity to receive a better understanding of how Artificial Intelligence is used to advance the world today.
